background

I graduated from Queen's University in June 2023, completing my Bachelor of Arts (Honours) degree in Computing and the Creative Arts, with a focus in sound production & was the 2023 recipient of the Istvan Prize in Electroacoustics. During my time at Queen's, I gained a lot of useful skills in computer science, specifically in web development, and music production. I have always loved music since I was little, so being able to combine both coding and music for my studies was so exciting!

sound designer

I specialize in video game soundtrack production and like to experiment with electroacoustic audio curation. I worked on GnomeFire Labs' design team during the summer of 2023, assisting on sound development for their upcoming game, RaceTrap. During my undergraduate studies, in my electrouacoustic composition class I dabbled with the concept of generative audio using ChucK to create algorithmic-based sounds. As of recent, I have been creating my own music for fun, in addition to helping friends with their audio-related projects. Currently, I'm helping a friend create a soundtrack for their web application!

creative developer

I have a passion for web development and app development. Fun fact: this whole website was hand-coded by me from scratch! That's right, no templates and no website builders. I wanted to challenge myself and learn new CSS skills, and I had a really fun time creating it! I also like to make web apps with my partner in my spare time! We recently created a daily 'wordle-style' game for guessing hex codes called Hexcodle. Currently, I am assisting friends by helping them design and bring their own websites to life! In addition to coding, I enjoy creating art, whether that be making a fun digital design in Illustrator, making pixel art animations, or drawing on my notebooks. Everything is my canvas!
