background

I graduated from Queen's University in June 2023, earning my Bachelor of Arts (Honours) degree in Computing and the Creative Arts, with a focus on sound production. I was honored to receive the 2023 Istvan Prize in Electroacoustics. During my time at Queen's, I acquired valuable skills in computer science, specifically in web development, and music production. My lifelong love for music found a perfect intersection with coding during my studies, making the learning experience incredibly exciting!

sound designer

I specialize in video game soundtrack production and enjoy experimenting with electroacoustic audio curation. In the summer of 2023, I contributed to GnomeFire Labs' design team, working on sound development for their upcoming game, RaceTrap. Currently, I'm collaborating with Torchlit Studios to bring their demo to life, adding audio for their idle screen In my undergraduate studies, I delved into generative audio concepts using ChucK to create algorithmic-based sounds in my electroacoustic composition class. Recently, I've been creating music for personal enjoyment and assisting friends with their audio-related projects.

creative developer

My passion for web development and app creation led me to hand-code this entire website from scratch—no templates or website builders involved. This endeavor allowed me to challenge myself, enhance my CSS skills, and, most importantly, have fun in the process! In my spare time, I collaborate with my partner to develop web apps. Our latest project is a daily 'wordle-style' game for guessing hex codes called Hexcodle Beyond coding, I indulge in creating art, whether it's crafting a digital design in Illustrator, making pixel art animations, or sketching in my notebooks. For me, everything is a canvas!
