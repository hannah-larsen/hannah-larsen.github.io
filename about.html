background

I graduated from Queen's University in June 2023, completing my Bachelor of Arts (Honours) degree in Computing and the Creative Arts, with a focus in sound production & was the 2023 recipient of the Istvan Prize in Electroacoustics. During my time at Queen's, I gained a lot of useful skills in computer science, specifically in web development, and also honed on my music production skills. I have always loved music since I was little, so being able to combine both coding and music for my studies was fantastic!

sound designer

I specialize in video game soundtrack production and like to experiment with electroacoustic audio curation. I worked on GnomeFire Labs' design team during the summer of 2023, assisting on sound development for their upcoming game, RaceTrap. During my undergraduate studies, in my electrouacoustic composition class I dabbled with the concept of generative audio using ChucK to create algorithmic-based sounds. As of recent, I have been creating my own music for fun, in addition to helping friends with their audio-related projects. My current project is assisting my friend by creating an OST for their web application!

creative developer

I have a passion for web development and like to make web apps with my partner in my spare time! We recently created a daily 'wordle-style' game for guessing hex codes called Hexcodle. In addition to coding, I also enjoy creating art, whether that be making a fun digital design in Illustrator, making pixel art animations, or drawing on my notebooks. Everything is my canvas!

interests

If I'm not making music or coding, you can find me learning languages on Duolingo or playing video games on my PC! I used to stream on Twitch during COVID and had such a fun time playing games, creating content for social media, and curating my Twitch channel theme, by hand. I also love to cook and experiement with new recipes whenever I get the chance.
