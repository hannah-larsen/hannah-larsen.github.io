background

I graduated from Queen's University in June 2023, completing my bachelor of arts (honours) degree in Computing and the Creative Arts, with a focus in sound production & was the 2023 recipient of the Istvan Prize in Electroacoustics. During my time at Queen's, I gained a lot of useful skills in computer science, specifically in web development, as well as honed in on my music production skills. I have always loved music since I was little. I started studying Royal Conservatory piano at 7, and finished my grade 8 requirements at 17.

sound designer

I specialize in video game soundtrack production and like to experiment with electroacoustic audio curation. I worked on GnomeFire Labs' design team during the summer 2023 period, assisting on sound development for their upcoming game, RaceTrap! During my undergrad, in my electrouacoustic composition class, I dappled with generative audio using ChucK to create algorithmic-generated sound. As of recent, I have been creating my own music for fun, in addition to helping friends with their audio projects. I've been helping a friend create background music for their web application.

creative developer

I have a passion for web development and like to make web apps with my partner in my spare time! We recently created a daily 'wordle-style' game for guessing hex codes called Hexcodle. In addition to coding, I also enjoy creating art, whether that be making a fun digital design in Illustrator, or drawing on my notebooks. Everything is my canvas!

interests
